About Me

Hi! My name is Alexa Bartlett, and I'm currently a third-year physics PhD student at UC Berkeley working with Martin White. My research interests broadly include cosmology theory and data analysis, large-scale structure, and weak lensing. I graduated from Swarthmore College with a BA in physics in 2023. Outside of physics, I enjoy making art and hanging out with my pet hamster, Rocky!
